About SunFish

SunFish transforms your cooking experience with intelligent recipe search. Whether you know the dish name or just have a few ingredients on hand, our platform helps you discover meals that match your cravings and kitchen stock.

Our mission is to make cooking accessible to everyone, from complete beginners to experienced home chefs. We believe that great meals should be easy to find, simple to prepare, and enjoyable to make.

From comforting classics to global flavors, every recipe in our database is clearly explained with step-by-step instructions. We've carefully curated our collection to ensure reliable results every time you cook.

What makes SunFish special is our focus on flexibility and intuition. Our smart search understands what you're looking for, even if you don't know the exact name of a dish. Just describe what you have or what you're craving, and we'll suggest perfect matches.

The platform is designed for exploration and experimentation. You can save favorite recipes, adjust serving sizes automatically, and even get substitution suggestions for ingredients you don't have on hand.

Whether you're meal planning for the week, looking for quick dinner ideas, or wanting to try something completely new, SunFish adapts to your needs and helps you enjoy the cooking process from start to finish.
